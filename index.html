COMP 1920 - Projects 1
Students learn to work in a collaborative environment and complete a small-scale sofware project.
Introduces design, management, and implementation concepts.
COMP 1536 - Intro to Web Development
This is a web design and programming course with an emphasis on good planning, interface design,
multimedia, client-side scripting with JavaScript, server-side scripting, and best practices.
This course will provide students with a solid understanding about how web applications are
constructed and deployed.
COMP 1510 - Programming Methods
this hand-on course is the foundation for all future programming courses and complements
COMP 1536 and COMP 1910. this course introduces the fundamental concepts of programming,
design, development, testing, debugging, as well as error-handling and problem solving.
COMM 1116 - Business Communications
This course will teach you how to be a professional and efficient communicator in the workplace.
You will write effective business correspondence and instructions and deliver a formal
oral presentation to your set.
COMP 1712 - Business Analysis and System Design
Busuness Analysis and systems design is the study of concepts, processes and tools that
professionals use to plan and develop information systems to industry standards. Studens learn how to ask
implicit questions, to create and document communication plans and to make better decisions.

COMP 1100 - CST Program Fundamentals
this course covers topics of importanace to new and continuing students on how to balance school and
related activities to enhance chances of success in their academic careers. In this course students will
learn a variety of different techniques to improve study habits, note taking, and time management.
COMP 1113 - Applied Mathematics
This course is partialy discrete mathematics, partially and introduction to linear equations.
The purpose of this course is to give a strong foundation for future technical and programming courses.
The course is divided into three parts: boolean algebra, number systems, and functions and linear equations.
